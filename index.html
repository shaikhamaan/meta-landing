METAMORPHOSIS

Metamorphosis is one the mega events of Walchand Linux Users' Group. This is a two day event comprising of exploring and learning two modern and important technologies. In this year we will be introducing Docker and Blockchain as those two technologies.

Register

Docker

Curious about containerization? Well here's an opportunity to learn about docker. Right from the need for docker to docker container and architecture, you'll learn docker in depth. And finally, the cherry on top - you'll have a practical demo of docker!

Blockchain

The small journey of Blockchain! Here you will get to dive in depth of Blockchain. The technical terms, benefits, usage and much more... Understand real-time applications, smart contracts, Solidity & perk of experiencing live demo.
